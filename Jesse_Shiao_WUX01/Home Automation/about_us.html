Established in 2017, Ace Electronics is New Zealand’s leading custom automation systems design and installation company.

As founding members of CEDIA – the International Custom Electronic Design and Installation Association, we undertake industry best practice to ensure the perfect experience from design, through installation, client training and ongoing support.

We place a strong emphasis on ongoing training and development for all staff members, including regular attendance at overseas training events. This ensures that your system benefits from the latest technology the world has to offer.

Our expert technicians are licensed by the New Zealand Ministry of Justice to install and service security systems.

AUTOMATION ASSOCIATES AWARDS

CEDIA is the Custom Electronic Design and Installation Association. This internationally recognised body is based in the USA. They promote professionalism and excellence in the AV industry globally.

For a New Zealand business to win an international award of this calibre is testament not only to the incredible clients we work with but to our to our world-class team and high end offering.
